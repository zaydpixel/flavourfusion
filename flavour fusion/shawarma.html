(S)hawarma Menu


Ingredients:
  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
    		•
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
    		•
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
    		•
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    		•
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marination:
In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken thighs, plain yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, ground cumin, paprika, ground turmeric, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix well until the chicken is evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.

Step 2 - Grilling:
Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers. Grill the chicken skewers for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and charred around the edges.

Step 3 - Assembling:
Warm the flatbreads or pita breads. Place a generous amount of grilled chicken onto each bread. Top with your choice of toppings such as sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, lettuce, and pickles.

Step 4 - Wrapping:
Roll up the bread tightly to enclose the filling, creating a shawarma wrap. Secure with parchment paper or foil if needed.

Step 5 - Serving:
Serve the shawarma wraps immediately with tahini sauce, hummus, or garlic sauce. Enjoy your delicious homemade shawarma!
