(K)ebab Menu


Ingredients:
  • 500g minced lamb
    		•
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    		•
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
    		•
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
    		•
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    		•
  • Salt, to taste
    		•
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marination:
In a large bowl, combine minced lamb, chopped onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped green chilies, chopped coriander leaves, ground cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, chili powder, turmeric powder, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well to combine all the ingredients evenly. Cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 2 - Shaping:
Preheat the grill or barbecue to medium-high heat. Take handfuls of the marinated mixture and shape them into long sausage-like shapes around the soaked wooden skewers. Press the mixture firmly to ensure it sticks to the skewers and holds its shape during grilling.

Step 3 - Grilling:
Place the skewers on the preheated grill or barbecue. Cook the kababs for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are evenly browned and cooked through. You can brush them with oil or ghee during grilling to keep them moist.

Step 4 - Serving:
Once cooked, remove the kababs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Serve hot kababs with mint chutney, sliced onions, and lemon wedges. They are perfect as a starter or as part of a main course.
