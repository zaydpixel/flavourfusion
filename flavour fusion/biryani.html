(B)iryani Menu


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
    		•
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
    		•
  • 1 cup yogurt
    		•
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
    		•
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
    		•
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
    		•
  • salt to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinating the chicken:
In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2 - Cooking the rice:
Wash basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

Step 3 - Cooking the chicken:
In a separate pan, heat ghee/oil. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add tomatoes, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook until tomatoes are soft. Add marinated chicken and cook until chicken is tender.

Step 4 - Layering the biryani:
In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the cooked rice at the bottom. Top with cooked chicken mixture. Sprinkle garam masala. Layer remaining rice on top. Garnish with fried onions (optional).

Step 5 - Dum cooking:
Cover the pot tightly with a lid or aluminum foil. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow flavors to meld together.

Step 6 - Serving:
Once done, gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita and salad.
